About Me

I'm Rhea—a student with a keen interest in both UI design and writing. I find great joy in the process of creating simple yet effective user interfaces that enhance the experience for users. Additionally, I am passionate about crafting well-organized written content that communicates ideas clearly and effectively. My ultimate goal is to seamlessly combine aesthetics with functionality in order to achieve outstanding results in both design and writing. By doing so, I hope to create work that not only looks appealing but also serves its purpose efficiently, making it both beautiful and practical.
