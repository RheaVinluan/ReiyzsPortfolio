Rhea Claire Angela A. Vinluan

I am currently pursuing a degree in Information Technology at Quezon City University, where I am deeply passionate about UI/UX design, writing, and the pursuit of continuous learning. Throughout my academic journey, I have dedicated myself to exploring these interests, which have become integral to my personal and professional growth. My involvement in campus journalism spans an impressive eight years. During my time in Junior High School, I honed my skills as a Feature Writer and eventually took on the responsibilities of Managing Editor. This experience set the foundation for my subsequent roles during my college years, where I served as Feature Editor from my first to third year. Now, in my fourth year, I am proud to contribute as the Associate Editor, a position that has allowed me to further refine my editorial and leadership skills. Beyond journalism, I have actively engaged in various leadership roles across both academic and extracurricular activities. I frequently take on the role of Project Manager for group projects, where I lead teams to successful outcomes. My contributions extend to the realms of UI/UX design and technical writing, where I apply my skills to enhance both the functionality and the user experience of projects. In addition to my editorial and leadership endeavors, I have developed basic expertise in frontend programming. I am continually working to improve these skills by pursuing relevant certifications and engaging in self-paced learning opportunities. This commitment to skill development underscores my dedication to staying current with industry trends and equipping myself with the necessary tools to thrive in the ever-evolving field of Information Technology.
