Iris

The farm irrigation and rainwater harvesting system features a modern layout. It provides easy access and a user-friendly setup.

Collaborative Project

Iris is an innovative rainwater harvesting monitoring and irrigation system designed for plants. It intelligently tracks rainwater collection and optimizes its use for watering plants, ensuring efficient water management and healthy plant growth. With its advanced monitoring features, Iris provides insights into water usage patterns, helping gardeners and farmers maintain a sustainable irrigation routine.
