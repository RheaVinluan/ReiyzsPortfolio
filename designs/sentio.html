Sentio

The UI design focuses on heart and mood monitoring. It elegantly combines functionality and aesthetics, ensuring user-friendly navigation.

Collaborative Project | Personal Output

Sentio is a mobile app designed to help users track their mood and journal their thoughts. It includes features that allow you to log daily emotions and reflect on your experiences. The app provides insightful feedback based on your entries, aiming to promote self-awareness and personal growth. Additionally, Sentio offers a mood summary feature, enabling users to monitor their emotional patterns over time.
