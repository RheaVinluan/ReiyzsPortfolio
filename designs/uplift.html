Uplift

The AI-powered mental health assistance website features a sleek interface. It offers intuitive navigation and user-friendly design.

Collaborative Project

Uplift is an AI-powered mental health assistant equipped with an AI chatbot, mood tracker, and journal. It helps users log their daily emotions and reflect on their experiences, offering insightful feedback that fosters self-awareness and personal growth. With its mood summary feature, Uplift enables users to monitor their emotional patterns over time, providing a comprehensive understanding of their mental well-being.
