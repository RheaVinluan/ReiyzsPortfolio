Brew Haven

The modern coffee shop website features a warm and inviting interface that reflects the cozy atmosphere of a café.

Personal Project

GitHub Repository: https://github.com/RheaVinluan/BrewHaven

BrewHaven is a coffee shop website designed to showcase the café's brand identity, featured beverages, and customer experience through a modern and user-friendly digital platform. The website includes sections for the café's story, menu highlights, testimonials, and calls-to-action, creating a seamless browsing experience for visitors. With responsive design and interactive elements, BrewHaven ensures accessibility and engagement across desktop and mobile devices while delivering a visually appealing representation of the coffee shop's atmosphere.
