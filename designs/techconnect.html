Tech Connect 2026

The technology conference website presents a modern and structured interface that captures the energy of innovation and knowledge-sharing within the tech community.

Personal Project

GitHub Repository: https://github.com/RheaVinluan/TechConnect2026

TechConnect 2026 is a technology conference website designed to showcase the event's brand identity, featured speakers, session schedules, and registration experience through a modern and user-friendly digital platform. The website includes sections for event highlights, keynote speakers, conference agenda, frequently asked questions, and calls-to-action, creating a seamless browsing experience for attendees. With responsive design and interactive elements, TechConnect 2026 ensures accessibility and engagement across desktop and mobile devices while delivering a visually compelling representation of the conference's innovative and collaborative atmosphere.
